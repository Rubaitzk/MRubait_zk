Problem Statement

Given an array of integers, find the maximum sum of a contiguous subarray. A subarray is a contiguous part of the array.

Input: [-2, 1, -3, 4, -1, 2, 1, -5, 4]

Output: 6

Explanation: [4, -1, 2, 1] has the largest sum = 6

Constraints

  • 1 ≤ nums.length ≤ 10^5
  • -10^4 ≤ nums[i] ≤ 10^4
